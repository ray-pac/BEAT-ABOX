WECARE PROGRAMMES

We work closely with non-profit organisations and elderly centres to run fully subsidised Cajon drumming programmes for seniors through the support of WeCare Arts Fund. Our 8-week programme equips seniors with fundamental drumming skills and prepares them for a mini showcase by the end of the last session. We will assign the most suitable instructor and provide the drums while you can focus on getting sign ups from the seniors.

Our structured 8-week programme offers a step-by-step approach to mastering the Cajon, starting with basic rhythms and gradually building confidence and skills. By the end of the journey, participants are prepared to perform in a mini showcase, celebrating their newfound abilities and sharing their joy with peers and loved ones.

We provide expert instructors and all necessary equipment, making it easy for you to focus on gathering participants.

  • Green Drumming WeCare Programme
  • 8 lesson x 1.5 hour each, held weekly
  • Date/Time to be discussed with partnering centre/organisation
  • Application to WeCare typically takes 8 weeks to approve

Looking for something sustainable, or more innovative? We also conduct recycled drumming lessons (bucket drums) for seniors across a range of physical and health conditions, check out our Green Drumming programmes.

HOLISTIC PROGRAMMES

We design and implememt holistic year-long programmes that help seniors to improve different aspects of their well-being: Physical, Emotional, Mental, Social and Psychological. Besides Cajon drumming and vocal training, we activate our network of educators and trainers to run other complementary activities such as Yoga, Meditation, Arts & Craft and Nutrition Talks.

In addition to Cajon drumming and vocal training, we collaborate with a network of educators to offer complementary activities such as yoga, meditation, arts & crafts, and nutrition talks.

Our activities go beyond just fitness—Cajon drumming and vocal training are key components that help stimulate cognitive function, improve mood, and encourage social interaction.

  • Gentle Yoga for Well-Being
  • Calm & Clarity: Guided Meditation
  • Hands-On Art: Crafting Wellness

Our programmes are designed for long-term impact, creating a supportive environment where seniors can continuously grow, engage, and improve their quality of life through a variety of meaningful experiences.

FESTIVE PROGRAMMES

Beyond our standard 8-week WeCare programmes, we provide refresher lessons for seniors and prepare them for internal performances during festive events, such as Christmas Year-End Party, Chinese New Year Party and National Day. Chat with us to find out more!

In addition to our standard 8-week WeCare programmes, we offer festive-themed programmes to engage seniors in seasonal celebrations. These programmes provide refresher lessons to help seniors stay active and connected through the power of music and performance.

Looking to bring more joy and energy to your festive event? Chat with us to learn how we can tailor our festive programmes to create memorable moments for your community.

  • Festive Cheer: A Christmas Soiree
  • A Year-End Celebration
  • Spring Fest: The Chinese New Year Party

These performances provide an opportunity for everyone to showcase their talents in front of a supportive audience.
