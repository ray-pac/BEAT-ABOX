BOX'OUT Series

We are the organiser of Singapore's largest Cajon Festival, BOX'OUT for three consecutive years (2013-2015). With over 1,000 participants turning up each year, it is a musical event where we come together to celebrate rhythm, unity and life. We bring uplifting music to fellow Singaporeans and empower them to pay-it-forward to beneficiaries with physical and mental disabilities, the elderly, and youth at-risk.

While we have taken a break from BOX'OUT, we are happy to explore new possibilities with potential collaborators. Chat with us today!

The impact of BOX'OUT goes beyond the beats—it creates a ripple effect of kindness and generosity, inspiring participants to bring the spirit of rhythm, unity, and giving into their everyday lives.

More than a festival, BOX'OUT is a platform to inspire and empower participants to spread joy and positivity. Attendees are encouraged to pay-it-forward by supporting beneficiaries, including those with physical and mental disabilities, the elderly, and youth at-risk.

Through uplifting performances and collective drumming, BOX'OUT fosters a sense of unity and togetherness. It’s a place where people from all walks of life come together to share the joy of music and give back to the community.
